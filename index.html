    About Us

    We Create affordable alternative to traditional electric car

    OSA Motors focuses on providing an affordable alternative to the overpriced electric vehicle in the United States market. Our service includes direct sales of electric converted vehicles as well as third party electric conversions.

    Our mission

    To provide an affordable converted electric-powered vehicle that any American household can afford.

    Our customers

    The target customers of OSA Motors are vehicle owners or prospective buyers who feel the necessity to switch to electric but are less financially available to purchase existing fully electric-powered vehicles due to price.

    Our product

    Our Lynx model vehicle have extremely price and capability unmatched by other manufacturers

    Why Choose US

    The electirc vehicle market is highly competitive, our well-crafted products allow us to standout from other competitors

    Company Information


    Legal
    Structure

    OSA Motors is a C Corporation, incorporated in Los Angeles, California.

    Government
    Regulations

    OSA Motors must meet all Federal and state regulations regarding motor vehicle safety. Specifically, the vehicle needs to pass a safety test and be certified to comply with Federal Motors Vehicle Safety Standards and other NHTSA regulations.

    Intellectual
    Property Rights

    OSA Motors is a trademarked name in the state of California, and we have filed for protection of our proprietary processes and other intellectual property, such as our designs and logo. We have also registered our domain name and parked relevant social media accounts for future use and to prevent the likelihood of someone impersonating one of our members.

    Our Process

    We Ensure Our Product to be the Best

    We are currently working on our MVP prototype. The prototype will include a converted vehicle from internal combustion to lithium-ion batteries, redesigned interior and exterior.

    Concept

    We have finished our concept on how to convert our internal combustion engine to lithum-ion powered.

    Develop

    We are currently waiting for conversion components from manufacturers, the target finishing date of the MVP prototype is 1st quarter of 2021.

    Test

    The MVP will require testing to accommodate with US safety regulations and refine the design before mass production. The testing phase will take approximately half year.

    Production

    The production is targeted in the late 4th quarter of 2021. The initial production is targeted to be 500 vehicles per year, which are all expected to be sold since there are no electric vehicles from other manufacturers or conversion service in the below $20,000 price range. The production will increase as the company expands.

    Our Marketing

    People must know what we do

    The electric vehicle industry is a rapidly growing industry. By many estimates, the electric vehicle market is estimated to grow to over 26 million units sold annually by 2030. Currently, no electric vehicles in the market are below the $30,000 mark in the United States.

    • Growth
    • Communicate
    • Sell
    • R&D
    • Lifecycle

    In order for the company to grow, OSA Motors will do the following:

    • Networking and continuing to cooperate with manufacturers in China.
    • Development for cutting edge technology to use in our vehicles.
    • Cooperation with federal and state authorities on vehicle safety.
    • Reach out car bloggers on popular media (Youtube, Facebook, Instagram, etc) to establish the OSA brand onto the market.
    • Establishment of an owner’s club to encourage brand loyalty and provide a welcoming environment for our customers.
    • Expanding production as sales increase.
    • Expanding the vehicle lineup to cover all types of family vehicles.

    OSA Motors will communicate with its customers by:

    • Regular meetings with the car community both online and in-person.
    • Encouraging bloggers on social media to preview the vehicles.
    • Providing advanced services online on both presale and post-sale services.

    OSA Motors will focus on establishing its brand and market base on social media by connecting with popular bloggers and reviewers. The company will attend motor shows to demonstrate our competitive pricing and services to the public, building upon the expectations/hype over our products. Website marketing will also play an important role, we will distribute our advertisements through popular search engines, such as Google. Our website will serve as the hub for our customers where they can see the advantages of our product over our competitors, and customize their vehicles with digital modeling before purchasing.

    The company is planning to conduct the following research and development:

    • A modular assembly kit that allows rapid conversion of different brands of vehicles by even minimally trained mechanics.
    • A potential hybrid between hydrogen fuel cell (both PEM and turbo) and a lithium-ion battery that allows for at least double the range of solely lithium-ion battery cars while only incurring a small increase in cost.
    • A website that is integrated with models in Unreal Engine allows the consumers to pick their customization for their cars on the website and view them in a realistic digital environment.

    All completed products are ready to be delivered upon the clients’ request. Additional time for production may be required for customization and special requirements.
